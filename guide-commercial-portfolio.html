How AI Changes Portfolio Management

1. Predictive Risk Detection

AI analyzes schedules daily, catching problems before they hit critical path:

  • Permit delays that will push steel delivery
  • Weather forecast affecting concrete pours
  • Subcontractor with 40% on-time rate scheduled for critical work
  • Material price spike coming (order now or pay 15% more)

2. Automated Payment Verification

AI reads PDFs, matches line items, flags discrepancies:

  • Compares quantities billed vs. approved schedule
  • Verifies lien waivers from all subs
  • Checks math on change orders
  • Confirms insurance certificates are current

3. Intelligent Scheduling

AI optimizes resource allocation across your portfolio:

  • Identifies when crews could move between projects
  • Suggests equipment sharing to reduce costs
  • Flags overcommitted resources before conflicts arise
  • Recommends schedule adjustments to improve cash flow
