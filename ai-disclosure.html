Your Rights Regarding AI Decisions

Right to Explanation

You can request an explanation of any AI-generated score, recommendation, or action that affects your account.

Right to Human Review

You may request human review of any automated decision that significantly affects your payments or project status.

Right to Contest

You may contest AI-generated risk scores or compliance flags if you believe they are inaccurate.

Right to Opt Out

You may disable automated payment recommendations and AI-driven notifications in your account settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact ai@buildsynq.com.

AI Limitations and Known Risks

We are transparent about the limitations of our AI systems:

  • Data quality: AI outputs are only as good as the underlying data. Incomplete project records, stale permit data, or missing contractor history can reduce accuracy.
  • Bias: Machine learning models trained on historical data may reflect historical patterns that disadvantage certain contractor groups. We actively audit our models for bias.
  • Confidence levels: All AI outputs include a confidence indicator. Low-confidence outputs should be treated with additional scrutiny.
  • Novel situations: AI systems perform less reliably on project types or jurisdictions with limited historical data.
  • Not legal or financial advice: AI outputs on BuildSynq do not constitute legal, financial, or professional advice.
