Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans at any time? ▼
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. When you upgrade, you get immediate access to new features. When you downgrade, changes take effect at the end of your billing cycle.
How do blockchain payments work? ▼
BuildSynq uses USDC stablecoins on the Solana blockchain for instant settlement. When a milestone is approved, payment can be released in under 400 milliseconds. USDC is pegged 1:1 to the US dollar — no crypto volatility. Available on Pro+ and Enterprise plans.
What happens with my data if I cancel? ▼
Your project data is retained for 90 days after cancellation. You can export everything at any time. Blockchain records are permanent and always accessible.
Is BuildSynq secure? ▼
BuildSynq uses enterprise-grade security including MFA authentication, database encryption (AES-256), SSL certificate pinning, biometric login, file virus scanning, and comprehensive audit logging. All payment data is processed through Stripe's PCI-compliant infrastructure.
Do contractors need to pay for BuildSynq? ▼
Contractors are invited to projects for free. They can view milestones, upload photos, communicate with homeowners, and receive payments without a paid account. If they want to manage their own projects and clients, they can subscribe to any plan.
How does government pricing work? ▼
Government plans are annual contracts starting at $50K/year for small cities (up to 50 users), $150K/year for large cities with blockchain audit trails (up to 200 users), and $500K+/year for state and federal agencies requiring air-gapped or GovCloud deployment. We support standard procurement processes including RFP responses and sole-source justification.
